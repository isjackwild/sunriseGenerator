Sunrise Generator

Love sunrises? Love lie-ins even more? Have a beautiful sunrise any time of day with the Sunrise Generator.

Save your sunrise

When you see one you like, just click it. It will open in a new window and from there you can right-click and select 'save as'. It'll be in the size of your device's screen, perfect to use as a wallpaper.

About

The Sunrise Generator was built by Jack Wild in 2014.
